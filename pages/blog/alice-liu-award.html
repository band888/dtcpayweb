Alice Liu Awarded FinTech Mentor of the Year at SFF FinTech Excellence Awards

November 2024

We are proud to announce that our CEO, Alice Liu, has been awarded the prestigious FinTech Mentor of the Year at the Singapore FinTech Festival (SFF) FinTech Excellence Awards. This recognition celebrates her outstanding contributions to nurturing the next generation of fintech talent.

A Commitment to Mentorship

Alice has been a dedicated mentor in the fintech ecosystem, sharing her expertise and experience with aspiring entrepreneurs and young professionals. Her guidance has helped numerous startups navigate the challenges of building successful fintech businesses.

Leading by Example

Under Alice's leadership, dtcpay has grown from a vision to a regulated Major Payment Institution serving thousands of businesses. Her commitment to innovation, regulatory compliance, and customer success has set an example for the entire industry.

The Singapore FinTech Festival

The Singapore FinTech Festival is one of the world's largest fintech events, bringing together the global fintech community to celebrate innovation and excellence. The FinTech Excellence Awards recognize outstanding individuals and organizations that are driving positive change in the industry.

Thank You

We congratulate Alice on this well-deserved recognition and thank all those who have been part of dtcpay's journey. This award is a testament to our commitment to building not just a successful company, but also a thriving fintech ecosystem.
