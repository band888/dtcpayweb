dtcpay Receives Green Light Letter for EMI License

January 2025

We are thrilled to announce that dtcpay has received the Green Light Letter for our Electronic Money Institution (EMI) license application. This significant milestone marks another step forward in our mission to provide comprehensive digital payment solutions.

What This Means for Our Customers

The EMI license will enable dtcpay to offer an expanded range of payment services, including the issuance of electronic money. This will allow us to better serve our customers with more flexible and innovative payment solutions.

Strengthening Our Regulatory Position

As a company already licensed by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) as a Major Payment Institution (MPI), this additional regulatory approval reinforces our commitment to operating within a fully compliant framework.

We remain dedicated to providing secure, efficient, and regulated payment solutions for businesses worldwide. This license approval is a testament to our team's hard work and our commitment to excellence in the digital payments space.

Looking Ahead

With this new capability, dtcpay is well-positioned to continue innovating and expanding our service offerings. We look forward to bringing new products and features to our customers in the coming months.

Thank you for your continued trust and support. Stay tuned for more exciting updates!
