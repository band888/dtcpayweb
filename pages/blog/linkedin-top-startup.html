dtcpay Recognised as LinkedIn Top Startup in Singapore

December 2024

We are honored and thrilled to announce that dtcpay has been recognized as one of LinkedIn's Top Startups in Singapore for 2024. This prestigious recognition highlights our growth, innovation, and impact in the fintech industry.

About the Recognition

LinkedIn's Top Startups list celebrates companies that are attracting attention and talent in their respective markets. The selection is based on four pillars: employment growth, engagement, job interest, and attraction of top talent.

Our Journey

Since our founding in 2019, dtcpay has been on a mission to bridge the gap between traditional finance and digital currencies. We have grown from a small team with a big vision to a regulated Major Payment Institution serving thousands of businesses across Asia and beyond.

Thank You

This recognition would not have been possible without our dedicated team, supportive partners, and loyal customers. We remain committed to innovating and delivering exceptional payment solutions that make a real difference for businesses worldwide.

As we continue to grow, we are excited about the opportunities ahead. Stay tuned for more updates as we work to shape the future of payments.
