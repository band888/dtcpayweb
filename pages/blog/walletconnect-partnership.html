dtcpay Partners with WalletConnect to Advance Onchain Payments

November 2024

We are excited to announce our strategic partnership with WalletConnect, the leading open-source protocol for connecting decentralized applications to mobile wallets. This collaboration marks a significant step forward in our mission to accelerate the adoption of onchain payments and stablecoin solutions.

Enhancing Payment Experiences

Through this partnership, dtcpay will integrate WalletConnect's technology to provide users with a seamless and secure way to connect their preferred crypto wallets to our payment solutions. This integration will enable businesses to accept payments from a wider range of wallet providers.

Benefits for Merchants

Merchants using dtcpay's solutions will benefit from:

  • Support for hundreds of wallet providers
  • Enhanced security through WalletConnect's proven protocol
  • Better user experience for crypto-native customers
  • Increased conversion rates for crypto payments

Looking Forward

This partnership reflects our commitment to building the infrastructure needed for mainstream stablecoin adoption. Together with WalletConnect, we are working to make digital payments more accessible, secure, and user-friendly for everyone.

Stay tuned for more updates as we roll out new features powered by this collaboration.
