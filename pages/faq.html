Payments

dtcpay supports major stablecoins including USDT (Tether), USDC (USD Coin), WUSD (Worldwide USD), and FDUSD (First Digital USD). We continue to add support for more stablecoins based on customer demand.

You can accept stablecoin payments through our POS+ solution for in-store payments, our Checkout solution for online payments, or PayByLink for remote payments. Contact our team to get started.

Our fees vary based on your business volume and the services you use. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden costs. Contact our sales team for a customized quote.

dtcpay Card

The dtcpay Card is a Visa card that allows you to spend your stablecoins at over 100 million locations worldwide. You can load your card with stablecoins and spend in local currencies anywhere Visa is accepted.

You can apply for a dtcpay Card through our mobile app or website. Complete the KYC verification process, and once approved, your card will be issued. Virtual cards are available immediately, while physical cards are shipped to your address.
