Everything you need to know about PayByLink.

What is PayByLink
PayByLink is a simple way to collect payments by creating and sharing a secure payment link with your customers. No coding required.
Can I collect custom information from my customers?
Yes, you can customize the checkout experience to collect additional information from your customers.
How am I notified of a successful payment?
You will receive instant email notifications for every successful payment. You can also view all transactions in your dashboard.
Can PayByLink accept multiple payment methods?
Yes, PayByLink supports stablecoins, credit cards, and various other payment methods.
Where can PayByLink be used?
PayByLink can be shared via email, SMS, WhatsApp, social media, or embedded in any digital communication channel.
